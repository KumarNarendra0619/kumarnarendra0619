# Hi there, I'm Dr. Narendra Kumar 👋

### Assistant Professor | Geographer | Spatial Demographer | GIS & Population Researcher

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Geography at Hemvati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University (A Central University), Srinagar, Uttarakhand, India. My work focuses on population dynamics, family welfare, health geography, spatial demography, land-use change analysis, and geospatial technologies for sustainable development. 

I combine Geography, GIS, Remote Sensing, Statistics, Spatial Analysis, and emerging AI technologies to address challenges in population studies, environmental management, and regional development.  :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
