### Assistant Professor | ICSSR-Post Doctoral Fellow | Global Open Mapping GURU | Geodemographer | Traveller by Hobby | Poet, Lyrics by Birth

I am an **Assistant Professor** in the **Department of Geography** at **Hemvati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University (A Central University)**, Srinagar, Uttarakhand, India. My Research focuses on **population dynamics** and their spatial–environmental dimensions, including fertility transition, migration, health geography, and demographic indicators. It also includes **geospatial modelling, land-use change, water resource sustainability, and open geospatial data systems**. The work applies GIS, remote sensing, **statistics, spatial analysis**, and **AI tools to support studies in population change**, environmental management, and regional development **data-driven policy and planning**.
